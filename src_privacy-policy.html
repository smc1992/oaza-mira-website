Privacy Policy

1. Controller

  • AEON Association for Education and Organization of Nexus between Generations Geneva, Switzerland
  • 📧 E-mail: contact @ oaza-mira.app

2. Data we collect

We process personal data only to the extent necessary to provide our services or respond to inquiries, including:

  • Contact and registration details (name, e-mail, phone number)
  • Information about orders or requests
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, access time)

3. Purpose and legal basis

Your data is processed to:

  • operate our website and app (Art. 6 (1)(f) GDPR)
  • enable use of our services (Art. 6 (1)(b) GDPR)
  • respond to inquiries (Art. 6 (1)(f) GDPR)
  • comply with legal obligations (Art. 6 (1)(c) GDPR)
  • send newsletters or marketing information only with your explicit consent (Art. 6 (1)(a) GDPR)

4. Use of HubSpot

Your data is used for:

  • We use HubSpot, a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform from HubSpot Inc. (HubSpot Ireland Limited, HubSpot House, One Sir John Rogerson’s Quay, Dublin 2, Ireland).
  • HubSpot helps us manage contacts, communications, email marketing, automation, and analytics.
  • Data is stored on HubSpot servers in the EU (Ireland). In rare cases, data may be transferred to the USA — only under EU Standard Contractual Clauses or the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.
  • A data processing agreement (DPA) pursuant to Art. 28 GDPR has been concluded with HubSpot
  • More information: https://legal.hubspot.com/privacy-policy
  • Legal basis: legitimate interest (Art. 6 (1)(f) GDPR) and, where required, consent (Art. 6 (1)(a) GDPR).

Our website uses only technically necessary cookies. If analytics or marketing cookies are used, we will ask for your consent first. You can withdraw your consent at any time via cookie settings.

7. Storage period

Personal data is kept only as long as necessary for the respective purpose or as required by law.

8. Your rights

 You have the right to access, rectify, delete, restrict processing, data portability, object to processing and withdraw consent at any time.To exercise these rights, send an e-mail to contact @ oaza-mira.app.
You may also contact the competent data protection authority in Switzerland or your country of residence.

9. Data Security

We protect personal data through technical and organizational measures against loss, misuse and unauthorized access (e.g. SSL encryption, access restrictions, regular backups).
