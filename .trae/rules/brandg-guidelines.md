## 🕊️ What is Oaza Mira?

**Oaza Mira** is a digital mediation platform for grave care and remembrance services. It enables families, institutions, and organizations to **commission and manage** grave maintenance, floral decoration, and memory sharing digitally – regardless of location.

Local, verified service providers (e.g. florists, caretakers) fulfill the orders with photo documentation and transparent workflows.

The platform serves as a **dignified, modern solution** for maintaining resting places and remembering loved ones – especially useful for those who are far away, elderly, or have limited mobility.

---

## ✅ What Problem Does Oaza Mira Solve?

- **Distance and Mobility**: Relatives abroad or with mobility challenges cannot regularly visit graves.
- **Trust and Transparency**: Traditional grave care lacks documentation or service proof.
- **Coordination Hassles**: Finding, booking, and communicating with local providers is inefficient.
- **Institutional Complexity**: Managing multiple graves or memorials across locations is difficult without digital tools.

Oaza Mira solves this with a **centralized platform** that provides peace of mind, visual proof, and reliable service – all managed through an app.

## 🎯 Who is it For?

### 👪 Private Customers (B2C)

Families and individuals – especially from the **Croatian diaspora** – seeking reliable grave care and digital remembrance tools.

> Use Case: A daughter in Canada sends fresh flowers to her father's grave in Croatia, with photo confirmation.
> 

---

### 🏢 Companies & Associations (B2B)

Organizations managing graves of employees, veterans, historical figures, or memorial sites.

> Use Case: A city government commissions recurring care for war memorials with centralized invoicing and documentation.
> 

---

### 🏛️ Institutions

Churches, municipalities, and local administrations needing structured grave and monument maintenance.

> Use Case: A parish manages holiday decorations and state visit preparations through a shared dashboard.
> 

---

## 🚫 What Oaza Mira is *NOT*

- ❌ A service provider – **Oaza Mira does not perform services itself**. It connects users with local, verified professionals.
- ❌ A florist shop – It is not a product marketplace or bouquet store.
- ❌ A social media platform – The memory-sharing feature is intimate and purpose-driven, not for public broadcasting.
- ❌ A religious service – It is strictly **neutral** and open to all beliefs.

## 🎯 Vision

To create a dignified, accessible, and modern way for people to care for graves and remember loved ones — regardless of distance, age, or ability.

## 🛠️ Mission
